Oromia Development Association Special Boarding School

ODA is one of non governmental organization found in Ethiopia which focus on community development and charity! ODA is a local and membership-based established in 1993 to improve the lives of people in Oromia region through sustaible integrated community-based programs. ODA primarily focuses on livelihood diversification,community empowerment,public health promotion, education, provision of potable water, environmental protection and promoting voluntarism to contribute to the development of the region. nowadays ODA is working on education sector by building schools in different parts of Oromia and also by opening boarding schools in Adama and Bishoftu. Education program is one of the key thematic areas that ODA has paid due attention in which remarkable achievements have been registered within the strategic plans.
The main objective of this effort is to promote access and quality of education in complementary to the government effort through filling the critical gaps that the government could not address the overwhelming demand of the education service. Accordingly, in 2006 E.C, ODA Boarding School was established in accordance with Article 6(c) of the Oromia Development Association regulation. The Boarding School aspires to be center of change and development ,a catalyst for progress in science and technology, addressing the immediate and long term demand of national economy, innovative research and education.
Therefore, ODA Boarding School has made progress that has registered dramatic results at regional and national level. ODA-Special Boarding schools are envisaged to provide education that ensures all round development of the students under the guidance and supervision of skilled and high level educational and pedagogical experts. Our moto is unity for development!

Mission

Our Mission is providing high quality education, nature talent and innovation to develop moral and spritual well being of each student in preparation for life of service.

Vision

Our Vision is to see ODA Boarding school becoming the fountain of best minds to produce dedicated future leaders, professional who serve humanity in a diverse national and global society.
